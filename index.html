Who we are

We are graduate students majoring in marketing and curation, amateur photographers and art lovers. Birds in the Cage was decided on a whim during the gathering.

Introduction of Birds in the Cage

Birds in the Cage is a modern photographic work, which reflects the restraint and psychological discomfort that people receive during the epidemic, including the setting of bird cage and other obscure expressions

What we are trying to tell

Through shooting indoors and outdoors, the cold indoors will express the impression of bondage. Through outdoor shooting, we will express the changes in people's hearts before and after the epidemic, as well as the redemption of love and friendship to mankind.
